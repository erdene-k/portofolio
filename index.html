Profile

Most of my work is invisible — and that's the point. Good backend engineering means payments clear, loans disburse, and documents generate without anyone thinking about how.

I started in frontend, shipping a mobile loan app to 500,000+ users, then moved deeper into the stack: payment gateway integrations, document generation systems, PDF data-extraction middleware for SME credit scoring, and a debt-collection platform. That full-stack path means I design APIs the way the people consuming them wish they were designed.

Today I'm a Senior Backend Developer at Tavan Bogd NBFI, where I lead code reviews, set system standards, and build the services that keep a non-bank financial institution running.
